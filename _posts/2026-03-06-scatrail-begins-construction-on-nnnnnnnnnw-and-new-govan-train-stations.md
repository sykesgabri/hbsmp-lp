![New Govan Train Station under construction.](/assets/images/hbnn/2026-03-06/newgovanunderconstruction.webp)

In a move landmarking the birth of New New New New New New New New New Whitlawburn, as well as New Govan, ScatRail immediately begins construction on the two town's respective train stations, before much else if anything is built in either town.

When asked for comment, ScatRail representitive Sabriel Gykes, who asked to remain anonymous, stated the following:
<blockquote>
    "That's how committed we are, we weren't going to wait for the rest of the town to build up and leave us a spot of their choosing, we chose our spot so they could build up around us. By taking action this early we have ensured that we can build the best stations within our ability, and start early on delaying people's journeys as they travel from the pub to their homes that as of yet are not even built."
    <span class="quote-attr">- Sabriel Gykes, ScatRail representitive, 2026</span>
</blockquote>
