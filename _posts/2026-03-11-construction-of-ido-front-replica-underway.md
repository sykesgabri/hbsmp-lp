![The colossal structure as viewed from the outside](/assets/images/hbnn/2026-03-11/idofront.webp)

Construction has begun on a replica of Made in Abyss' Ido Front facility over a thousand blocks north of New Govan. The colossal structure resembles a cylindrical monolith over 100 blocks in diameter, towering above the clouds all the way up to build limit, with a gap in the cylinder around 1/4 of the way up, leaving what is still a massive wall resembling a colloseum, with a large monolith appearing to magically float above it. Seventeen blue and purple beacons shine light through this gap, adding to the imposing structure's almost supernatural feel.

The project is being built by two residents of NNNNNNNNNW, though for what purpose is unknown. Due to the nature of Ido Front in its source material, a scientific mega-facility on the fifth layer of the Abyss, used by Bondrewd to conduct ethically questionable research, as well as guard and regulate the only safe and well known passage into the sixth layer, what is considered by many to be the point of no return, with few characters surviving a return trip, it is theorised by some that this replica serves to guard and regulate the stronghold nearest the major settlements, NNNNNNNNNW officials have denied this, stating "no we made it cause it's sick as fuck, shut up".
